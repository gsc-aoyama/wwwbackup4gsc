Exchange Student Program

Welcome to the school of Global Studies and Collaboration(GSC), Aoyama Gakuin University(AGU)!

Aoyama Gakuin University accepts exchange students from partner universities.
If you are interested in coming to AGU as an exchange student (except for departmental exchange), please visit the International Exchange Center website for more details :
http://web.iec.aoyama.ac.jp/english/index.html

The school of Global Studies and Collaboration (GSC) develops students’ global perspectives with a focus on inter-Asian collaboration. Especially for international students, GSC’s Japan Studies Program offers opportunities to explore the multi-facets of Japan in English.
For more information, look at “Information for Exchange Student Program”.

Information for Exchange Student Program(PDF)

Departmental Exchange Program Application Materials ( for Inbound Students)

GSC has partners abroad. Students from the university partners below should submit the following applications before the required deadline.

Departmental Exchange Program partner universities are as follows:

  • Chulalongkorn University , Faculty of Arts
  • Thammasat University, Faculty of Liberal Arts
  • Mahidol University, Faculty of Liberal Arts

Please read “Eligibility and Application Instructions” before applying to the Exchange Student Program.
